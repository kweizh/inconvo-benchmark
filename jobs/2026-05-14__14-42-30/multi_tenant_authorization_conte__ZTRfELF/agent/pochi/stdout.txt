### `inconvo.yaml`
Declares the PostgreSQL integration with a `contextFilters` entry on the `orders` table that maps the `store_id` column to the conversation context key `store_id`. When Inconvo executes queries against `orders`, it will automatically append `WHERE store_id = <value from context>`, enforcing multi-tenant row-level isolation.

```yaml
integrations:
  - type: postgresql
    name: postgres
    tables:
      - name: orders
        contextFilters:
          - column: store_id
            contextKey: store_id
```
